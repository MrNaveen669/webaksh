# WebAksh Premium Agency Website Redesign

Redesign and upgrade the entire WebAksh website into a premium, modern, high-converting digital agency website.

Do not simply change colors or spacing. Improve the overall UI, UX, structure, interactions, storytelling, and lead generation.

The final result should feel comparable to top agency websites such as Linear, Stripe, Cuberto, Refokus, Lusion, and Awwwards-winning agency sites.

---

## Main Goal

WebAksh is a professional software and digital solutions agency helping businesses grow through:

* Website Development
* Mobile App Development
* E-Commerce Solutions
* UI/UX Design
* Digital Marketing
* Branding
* SEO Optimization
* Business Automation

The website should build trust, showcase expertise, and convert visitors into leads.

---

## Complete UI/UX Redesign

Implement a modern premium design system:

### Visual Style

* Dark luxury theme
* Glassmorphism cards
* Gradient accents
* Soft shadows
* Layered depth
* Smooth transitions
* Premium typography
* Spacious layouts
* Professional spacing system

### Animations

Use Framer Motion and GSAP.

Add:

* Smooth scroll animations
* Text reveal animations
* Staggered card animations
* Hover micro-interactions
* Parallax effects
* Section entrance animations
* Animated counters
* Magnetic buttons
* Smooth page transitions

Animations should feel professional and not excessive.
