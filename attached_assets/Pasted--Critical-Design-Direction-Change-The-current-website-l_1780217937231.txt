# Critical Design Direction Change

The current website looks more like a designer portfolio or typography experiment than a premium software agency.

The UI is visually impressive, but it does not feel like a real agency website built for businesses.

I want a complete refinement pass.

Do NOT redesign everything from scratch.

Keep the dark premium aesthetic, editorial design language, and modern feel.

However, improve the balance between design and business communication.

---

## Biggest Problem

The current typography is too large.

Examples:

* Hero headlines dominate the screen
* Contact headline dominates the section
* Large text creates too much empty space
* Content feels secondary

This makes the website feel like a design showcase rather than a company website.

Reduce oversized typography by approximately 25–35%.

Maintain strong typography but improve hierarchy and readability.

---

## Final Goal

When a business owner visits the website they should think:

"These people build serious software products and understand business."

Not:

"This is a cool design experiment."

The website should feel:

* Premium
* Professional
* Trustworthy
* Modern
* High-end
* Agency-focused
* Conversion-focused

Design quality should remain excellent, but business communication and credibility should be significantly improved.
